young love.

A letter for one of my greatest loves. Where do I start? Well, I'm all grown up now. Would I want to go back to the past and stay there forever? Hell yeah. Reminiscing about you is both so relaxing and painful, but more of the latter. I love thinking about how simple life was back then. It helps me realize how far I've come and how fast I'm growing up.

I miss you so much that it hurts. All I had to worry about was going home as fast as I could just so I wouldn't miss iCarly on Nickelodeon or Spongebob, depending on the day. You were so beautiful and naive back then. It was a blast with you, childhood.
